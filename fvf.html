The Best Three Movies of 2019

Release Date: August 30, 2019

Money Made: US $86.8 million and worldwide total of 185 million.

Description: American automotive designer Carrol Shellby and fearless British race car Ken Miles battle corporate interference, the laws of physics and their own personal demons to build a revolutionary vehicle for the Ford Motor Co. Together, they plan to compete against the race cars of Enzo Ferrari at the 24 Hours of Le Mans in France in 1966.
